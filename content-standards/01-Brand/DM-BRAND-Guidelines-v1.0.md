# DnyanMitra Brand Guidelines

This document defines the core brand identity, vision, values, and communication standards for **DnyanMitra**, a digital B2B school procurement platform developed by **DASP Digital**.

---

## 🌟 Brand Mission & Vision

### Mission
To digitise and simplify the B2B procurement lifecycle for educational institutions across India, ensuring transparency, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness by connecting them with certified, pre-vetted vendors.

### Vision
To become the definitive national infrastructure and single source of truth for educational procurement, enabling institutions to focus on learning rather than logistics.

---

## 🛡️ Core Brand Values

1. **Transparency**: Clear quoting, side-by-side comparison, and no hidden vendor margins.
2. **Quality (Excellence)**: Only pre-vetted, high-performing suppliers with verified academic track records are onboarded.
3. **Efficiency**: Cutting RFP-to-delivery lead times from months to days.
4. **Empowerment**: Providing school administrators with tools to make data-driven, budget-conscious purchasing decisions.

---

## 🗣️ Brand Personality & Tone of Voice

DnyanMitra's personality is **Professional, Reassuring, Clear, and Indian-centric**. 

### 1. Reassuring (For School Administrators)
- **Example**: 
  - *Avoid*: "Buy lab equipment cheap here!"
  - *Use*: "Source certified laboratory equipment compliant with the latest CBSE guidelines, with multiple side-by-side vendor quotes."

### 2. Efficient & Business-like (For Vendors)
- **Example**:
  - *Avoid*: "We would love to help you grow your business when you get a chance."
  - *Use*: "Review active RFPs from verified institutions and submit your competitive bid directly through the DnyanMitra Vendor Dashboard."

---

## 🎨 Visual Brand Guidelines

### Brand Color Palette
We use a premium, trust-oriented color system representing education and modern digital efficiency.

- **Primary Blue (`#0B3C5D`)**: Deep Navy - represents trust, stability, and professionalism.
- **Accent Marigold (`#D9B310`)**: Warm Gold/Yellow - represents knowledge, warmth, and innovation.
- **Secondary Slate (`#328CC1`)**: Soft Digital Blue - represents technology and efficiency.
- **Neutral Charcoal (`#1D2731`)**: Slate/Charcoal - for high-contrast readable body text.

### Typography
- **Primary Typeface**: **Inter** (for body text, labels, and documentation).
- **Headings Typeface**: **Outfit** (for headers, landing page banners, and display text).

## 📖 Standard Brand Vocabulary

| Term | Definition | Avoid |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **DnyanMitra Platform** | The entire digital procurement ecosystem. | DnyanMitra website, the shop. |
| **Pre-Vetted Vendor** | A supplier verified for legal, financial, and quality standards. | Seller, shopkeeper, partner. |
| **RFP (Request for Proposal)** | An official list of requirements created by an institution. | Query, list, order. |
| **School Partner** | An educational institution using DnyanMitra to procure goods. | Customer, client, user. |
