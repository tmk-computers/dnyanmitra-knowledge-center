# DASP Digital Suite Brand Expansion Framework

This document outlines the modular structure for launching new platforms under the **DASP Digital** ecosystem. By following this standard, future franchises, developers, and AI agents can adapt brand parameters for **KridaMitra**, **KrushiMitra**, and **ZilaValley** while maintaining core corporate values.

---

## 🏢 The DASP Digital Core Identity

DASP Digital Pvt. Ltd. builds technology platforms that digitise, standardise, and establish trust in sectors that are traditionally fragmented. Whether serving education (DnyanMitra), sports (KridaMitra), agriculture (KrushiMitra), or regional administration (ZilaValley), all services share three foundational corporate pillars:

1. **Vetting & Verification (Trust)**: Users are pre-screened to prevent fraud and maintain high execution standards.
2. **Transparent Marketplace (Efficiency)**: Open bidding systems that eliminate middleman markups.
3. **Regional Empowerment (Scale)**: Deploying local leadership (Taluka Heads, District Heads) to onboard partners and maintain regional networks.

---

## 🎨 Modular Platform Branding Blueprint

To launch a new platform, the branding and product teams swap out specific variables inside the DASP Digital core layout:

```text
[Platform Name] = [Prefix] + "Mitra" (or Regional Name)
[Primary Sector Color] = Sector Specific Hex Code
[Target Audience] = Primary Sector Participants
```

### Brand Parameter Matrix

## 🤝 Platform Expansion Naming Code

When adding documentation for a new platform, use the designated prefix in the naming convention:
- **Sports files**: `KM-SALES-Outreach-v1.0.md`
- **Agri files**: `KR-PROC-Fertilizer-Specs-v1.0.md`
- **Governance files**: `ZV-SOP-Contractor-KYC-v1.0.md`
