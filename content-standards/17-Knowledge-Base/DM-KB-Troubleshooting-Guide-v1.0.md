# How to Reset Your Vendor Password

If you have forgotten your DnyanMitra vendor portal password, or if your account is locked due to multiple incorrect attempts, follow this step-by-step resolution guide.

---

## 🎯 Summary Answer

Click **Forgot Password** on the login page to receive a secure reset link via your registered email and mobile number. The reset link is active for **10 minutes**.

---

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Navigate to the DnyanMitra login portal at `vendor.dnyanmitra.com`.
2. Locate the password input field and click on the **Forgot Password** link beneath it.
3. Enter your registered 10-digit mobile number or your registered business email address. Click **Submit**.
4. Check your mobile messages for a 6-digit One-Time Password (OTP) or check your email inbox for the reset link.
5. Enter the OTP on the verification screen, or click the link in your email.
6. Enter a new password. Your password must contain:
   - At least 8 characters.
   - At least one uppercase letter (A-Z).
   - At least one number (0-9).
   - At least one special symbol (e.g. `@, #, $, &`).
7. Click **Confirm Reset**. Log in using your new credentials.

---

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### I did not receive the OTP. What should I do?
First, verify that your mobile network signal is stable. Wait for 60 seconds, then click **Resend OTP**. If you still do not receive it, verify that your phone is not blocking SMS notifications from DASP Digital shortcodes.

### The link in my email says "Link Expired".
For security purposes, reset links expire after **10 minutes**. If your link has expired, navigate back to `vendor.dnyanmitra.com` and trigger a fresh request.
