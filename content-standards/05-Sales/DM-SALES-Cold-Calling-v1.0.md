# Cold Calling Sales Script

This document establishes the conversational framework, pitch structure, and objections handling guidelines for DnyanMitra tele-sales teams and automated calling systems.

---

## 🎯 Target Persona: School Principal / Trustee

- **Core Goal**: Secure a 10-minute online Zoom walkthrough of the digital procurement dashboard.
- **Tone**: Formal, respectful, objective, and solution-driven.

---

## 📞 Conversational Pitch Flow

### 1. Introduction & Permission Hook
> "Good morning, Respected Sir/Madam. Am I speaking with [Principal/Trustee Name], the Administrator of [School Name]?  
> My name is [Sales Rep Name] calling from DnyanMitra, developed by DASP Digital. I am calling to share how we assist CBSE and State Board schools in Pune in digitising their lab and classroom procurement. May I take two minutes of your time?"

### 2. The Pain Point & Value Prop
> "Most school leaders we consult with find that collecting furniture and equipment quotes is highly manual—requiring staff to call multiple vendors individually, waiting weeks for PDFs, and often ending up with single-source price inflation.  
>  
> DnyanMitra provides a B2B school procurement marketplace. You post your list of requirements once, and receive at least three competitive, pre-vetted bids side-by-side within 48 hours. Our partner schools save an average of 15% on their capital expense budgets."

### 3. The Call to Action (CTA)
> "We would love to show you a quick 10-minute online walkthrough of the dashboard this Thursday at 11:00 AM IST. Would this slot suit you, or would Friday afternoon be better?"

---

## 🛡️ Objections Handling Matrix

| Objection | Recommended Response |
| :--- | :--- |
| **"We already have local vendors we trust."** | *"That is excellent. In fact, DnyanMitra allows you to invite your existing trusted vendors to the portal so they can bid alongside national suppliers. This maintains your relationships while giving your trust board transparent audit records."* |
| **"Is there a registration fee for schools?"**| *"No, registration and RFP posting is completely free for educational institutions. We only verify your school's board affiliation before activation."* |
| **"We don't buy anything online."** | *"Understood. DnyanMitra is not an online retail store. It is a bidding portal. You receive competitive quotes digitally, but the delivery, inspection, and payment follow standard institutional PO workflows."* |
