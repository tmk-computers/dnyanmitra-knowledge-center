# Trustee Board Engagement Guidelines

This document outlines the protocols, governance requirements, and board resolution formats for adopting DnyanMitra digital procurement within educational trusts.

---

## 🛡️ Governance Requirements

Educational trusts and societies operate under strict fiduciary responsibilities. DnyanMitra sales and onboarding teams must ensure trust interactions comply with these core regulations:

1. **Audit trail integrity**: All quotations, bidding timestamps, vendor verification profiles, and scoring sheets must be exportable in PDF format to back up annual trust audits.
2. **Conflict of Interest Disclaimer**: Bidding vendors must submit a digital disclaimer confirming they share no family or financial ties with the purchasing institution's active trustees.
3. **Flat Transaction Service Charge**: Highlight that DnyanMitra operates on a clear, flat platform charge or transactional fee, preventing hidden markups or kickbacks.

---

## 🗣️ Communication Protocol

- **Language Style**: Always write formally. Use honorific titles such as "Honourable Chairperson", "Revered Trustee", or "Dr./Mr./Mrs. [Last Name]".
- **Evidence-Based Reporting**: Provide data-backed comparison tables instead of speculative opinions. Highlight verified vendor reviews and past delivery ratings.

---

## 📜 Board Resolution Template for Platform Adoption

When a trust transitions its school sourcing to DnyanMitra, they must pass a formal resolution. Provide the trustee panel with this template:

```text
RESOLUTION PASSED AT THE BOARD MEETING OF {{TRUST_NAME}} HELD ON {{DATE}}

"RESOLVED THAT the Trust adopts the DnyanMitra B2B Digital Procurement Platform (developed by DASP Digital) as the official portal for all school sourcing, bidding, and vendor management.

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT all procurement exceeding INR {{PROCURMENT_LIMIT}} must be put out for digital bid via the DnyanMitra platform, requiring at least three competitive quotes from pre-vetted vendors before purchase order release.

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT {{OFFICER_NAME}}, in their capacity as {{OFFICER_DESIGNATION}}, is hereby authorised to sign and execute all agreements and documentation associated with the platform adoption on behalf of the Trust."
```
