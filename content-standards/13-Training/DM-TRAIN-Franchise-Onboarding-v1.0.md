# Franchise Partner Onboarding Training

This training module prepares regional franchise coordinators (Taluka Heads) to deploy and run DnyanMitra services in their zones.

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this training module, coordinators will be able to:
- Navigate the DnyanMitra partner dashboard.
- Perform a physical site verification checklist for local vendors.
- Guide school administrators through posting their first RFP.

---

## 📚 Section 1: Explain (Core Principles)

DnyanMitra expands through localized franchise partners. Our local team represents trust. As a franchise coordinator, your duty is to prevent fraudulent listings.
- **Rules of Vetting**: We never activate a vendor profile solely based on online applications. A physical spot verification of the vendor's stock, facility, or shop is mandatory.
- **RFP Lifecycles**: A school posts requirements -> Vetted vendors receive notifications -> Bids are compiled -> Bids close in 48 hours.

---

## 💻 Section 2: Demonstrate (Dashboard Walkthrough)

*Prerequisites: Log in with coordinator credentials to `partner.dnyanmitra.com`.*

1. **Verify KYC Drafts**: Open the **Pending KYC** tab. Review the uploaded GSTIN certificate and PAN card.
2. **Assign Vetting Task**: Click **Assign Vetting Task** and allocate to the local Taluka Head.
3. **Approve Status**: Once the physical inspection report is uploaded, select **KYC Approved** to activate the vendor's bid capability.

---

## ✏️ Section 3: Apply (Simulation Exercise)

Onboard a mock vendor named *"Pune Academic Furniture"* using these parameters:
- **GSTIN**: `27AAACP0123A1Z1` (Verify active status on `gst.gov.in`).
- **Site Photos**: Upload three verified shop photos.
- **Bank mandate**: Verify IFSC and pennydrop success status.

---

## ⚖️ Section 4: Check (Knowledge Assessment)

Trainees must pass this scenario quiz before receiving field access:

### Quiz Question
- **Scenario**: A vendor uploads a clear GST certificate but refuses to host a brief 5-minute site-verification visit or video check.
- **Question**: What is the correct protocol?
- **Options**:
  - A) Approve the account since the tax document is clear.
  - B) Reject the profile and set status to "Flagged - Site Check Refused". *(Correct Answer)*
  - C) Call the vendor's banker to ask for feedback.
  - D) Delete the vendor's data immediately.
- **Rationale**: Physical or verified visual vetting is a non-negotiable trust safety barrier for DnyanMitra and DASP Digital platforms.
