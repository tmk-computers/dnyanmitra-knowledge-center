# Homepage Layout Structure

This document outlines the modular blocks for the main DnyanMitra homepage. AI models can parse these parameters to generate matching copy, layout adjustments, and call-to-actions.

---

## 🗂️ Landing Page Blueprint

```yaml
page:
  title: "DnyanMitra - B2B School Procurement Marketplace"
  layout_version: "1.0.0"
  modules:
    - id: "hero_section"
      headline: "Digitise and simplify school procurement"
      subheadline: "Get at least three competitive quotes from pre-vetted vendors side-by-side in 48 hours."
      cta: "Register Your School"
    - id: "problem_grid"
      items:
        - title: "Inflated Quotations"
          body: "Single-source procurement leads to budget leakages and high margins."
        - title: "Delayed Shipments"
          body: "Fragmented logistics disrupt CBSE syllabus timelines."
    - id: "solution_pillars"
      items:
        - title: "Transparent Sourcing"
          body: "Competitive digital bidding ensures fair market prices."
        - title: "KYC Verified Vendors"
          body: "DASP Digital verifies legal, tax, and delivery capabilities in advance."
    - id: "closing_cta_banner"
      headline: "Ready to optimize your next procurement cycle?"
      cta: "Book a Demo Walkthrough"
```
