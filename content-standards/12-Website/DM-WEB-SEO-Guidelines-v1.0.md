# SEO Copywriting Guidelines

This document defines the SEO standards, meta tags, and character length limits for all public web pages of DnyanMitra and other DASP Digital portals.

---

## 🔍 Meta Tag Specifications

AI engines and copywriters must ensure the header tags of every page align with these limits:

| Tag | Characters Limit | Requirements | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Title Tag** | 50 – 60 | Must include target keyword first, followed by brand suffix | `School Procurement Portal India \| DnyanMitra` |
| **Meta Description** | 120 – 150 | Must summarize page value and end in an active call to action | `Compare competitive bids from verified Indian school suppliers. Streamline smart classroom and school furniture procurement with DnyanMitra. Register today!` |
| **H1 Tag** | < 70 | Exactly **one** per page. Must contain the primary SEO keyword | `# Streamline school procurement with DnyanMitra` |

---

## 🎨 Image ALT Text Guidelines

All images must have an `alt` attribute representing the exact content of the image. Never leave it blank or use generic titles.
- **Rules**:
  - Keep under 125 characters.
  - Integrate secondary keywords naturally.
  - Describe the visual layout clearly.
- **Example**:
  - *Correct*: `alt="DnyanMitra platform dashboard displaying side by side school vendor bidding comparison"`
  - *Incorrect*: `alt="Dashboard"` or `alt="screenshot1.png"`

---

## ⚖️ Keyword Density Standards

To prevent search engine keyword stuffing penalties:
- **Primary Keyword Density**: Maintain a target density of **1.0% to 1.5%** of the page word count (e.g. 10 to 15 occurrences in a 1,000-word blog post).
- **Secondary Keywords**: Distribute synonyms naturally in H2 and H3 subheadings.
