## 🏛️ Executive Summary

This checklist ensures that every newly appointed Taluka Head completes all legal compliance steps and receives the necessary software credentials and marketing assets before launching DTC field activities.

---

## 🗂️ Onboarding Execution Checklist

### 1. Compliance & Legal Documents
- [ ] **Partnership Agreement**: Executed and signed by both parties.
- [ ] **Identity Vetting**: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, and GST registration certificate (if applicable) uploaded.
- [ ] **Bank Details**: Verified bank mandate form with cancelled cheque (Pennydrop test complete).
- [ ] **Physical DTC Site Visit Report**: Uploaded and signed off by the District Head.

### 2. CRM & Communication Setup
- [ ] **CRM Account Provisioned**: User login credentials generated.
- [ ] **Official Email Account**: Active `@dnyanmitra.com` coordinator email address.
- [ ] **Official Communication Groups**: Onboarded to the district WhatsApp and Telegram groups.
- [ ] **Partner Portal Access**: Credentials granted to the knowledge base library.

### 3. Sales & Marketing Collateral
- [ ] **Branding Kit**: High-resolution DnyanMitra logo, standee vector files, and brochure templates.
- [ ] **Sales Presentation Material**: Digital slides, school audit sheets, and script playbooks.
- [ ] **Business Card Layouts**: Branded business card designs showing partner credentials.
- [ ] **Product Catalogue & Price Sheet**: Access to updated catalogues.

---

## 🎓 4. Initial Training Milestones

Confirm completion of the first-week training agenda:

- **Day 1: CRM & Administrative Tools Training**
  - [ ] Lead creation, route mapping, and report generation modules.
- **Day 2: Marketplace Vendor Panel Walkthrough**
  - [ ] Vendor onboarding process, bank details validation, and product listing reviews.
- **Day 3: Product Sourcing & Technical Features Training**
  - [ ] Smart boards, biometric devices, and school ERP features study.
- **Day 4: Field Training**
  - [ ] Joint school visits and demo presentations with the District Head.

---
