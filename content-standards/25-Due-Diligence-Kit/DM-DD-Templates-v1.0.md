## 🏛️ Executive Summary

This document compiles the standardized operational templates required by District Heads to log meeting progress, follow up with candidates, size territory risk, and request corporate sign-offs.

---

## 📂 Reusable Templates Index

### 1. Meeting Minutes Template (MoM)
```markdown
# Meeting Minutes: Taluka Partner Selection

- **Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **Taluka**: {{taluka_name}}
- **Attendees**: {{candidate_name}} (Candidate), {{district_head_name}} (District Head)
- **Meeting Type**: [1st Demo / 2nd Planning Workshop / 3rd Onboarding]

### Discussion Summary
- [Briefly note points discussed: network size, computer lab space, etc.]

### Action Items
1. Candidate to submit PAN & GSTIN details by YYYY-MM-DD.
2. District Head to share local school list by YYYY-MM-DD.
```

---

### 2. Candidate Feedback Form
```markdown
# Candidate Feedback Form (District Head Notes)

- **Candidate Name**: {{candidate_name}}
- **Training Centre Name**: {{institute_name}}
- **DTC Address**: {{office_address}}

### Vetting Feedback
1. **Network Rating (1-5)**: {{rating_network}}  
   *Notes*: [Note local school contacts]
2. **Technical Skill (1-5)**: {{rating_technical}}  
   *Notes*: [Note experience with ERPs/smart boards]
3. **Commitment Status**: [Highly Interested / Hesitant / Not a fit]
```

```markdown
  - {{school_name_1}} (Target product: {{product_1}})

```

### 6. Meeting Follow-up Email Template
```text
Subject: Next Steps - DnyanMitra Taluka Business Planning Workshop

Dear Mr. [Candidate Last Name],

Thank you for your active participation in our Taluka Business Planning Workshop today. We appreciated learning more about your computer training institute and discussing the technology infrastructure roadmap for [Taluka Name].

As discussed, I have attached the DnyanMitra Due Diligence Kit to help you review our operational standards, revenue-sharing commissions, and onboarding timeline. 

You can access the kit documents directly here:
- DnyanMitra Overview: DM-DD-DnyanMitra-Overview-v1.0.md
- Revenue Sharing Model: DM-DD-Revenue-Sharing-Model-v1.0.md
- 12-Month Business Plan: DM-DD-12-Month-Business-Plan-v1.0.md

Please review these materials and let me know if today or tomorrow at 4:00 PM would be convenient for a quick call to answer any questions and schedule our final partnership onboarding session.

Best regards,

[District Head Name]
District Head - [District Name]
DASP Digital, Pune
```

### 7. Meeting Follow-up WhatsApp Script
```text
Hello Sir, thank you for your time at today's DnyanMitra Business Planning Workshop. It was great discussing the digital transformation opportunity for [Taluka Name]. 

I have shared the complete Due Diligence Kit with you via email, which contains detailed guides on the revenue-sharing model and our product catalog.

Please let me know if we can connect tomorrow at [Time] to discuss your feedback and answer any questions. Have a great evening! - [District Head Name], DASP Digital.
```
