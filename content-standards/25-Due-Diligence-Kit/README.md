# DnyanMitra Due Diligence Kit

Welcome to the **DnyanMitra Due Diligence Kit**. This kit is designed to provide prospective Taluka Heads and franchise partners with a transparent, business-focused, and detailed understanding of **DASP Digital** and the **DnyanMitra** ecosystem before finalising their partnership.

---

## 🎯 Purpose of the Due Diligence Kit

The primary objective of this kit is to facilitate informed decision-making. We believe in building trust through transparency, rather than using sales-driven marketing pitches. 

This kit contains:
- Company structure, core values, and technological architecture.
- Detailed worked examples of commission structures and revenue streams.
- The standard sales workflow and operational checklists.
- Objective checklists and scorecards used internally to evaluate candidate fit.

---

## 📅 When to Share the Kit

The kit must be shared with candidates **immediately following the second meeting (Taluka Business Planning Workshop)**. This gives the candidate ample time to review the material independently before the final selection and onboarding session.
