## 🏛️ Executive Summary

DASP Digital operates a highly localized B2B commission structure. Commissions are split across product categories to reward direct sales, local vendor support, and regional leadership. Rather than offering optimistic projections, this document provides realistic splits, worked transaction examples, and clear incentive structures.

---

## 📊 1. Commission Split Matrix

Every transaction routed through DnyanMitra distributes a platform margin share (commission) among regional DTC managers. The standard split of the **gross platform commission margin** (taken as 100% of the total margin available for distribution) is structured as follows:

```text
Gross Platforms Sourcing Margin (100%)
├── DASP Digital Platform Share (40%)
├── State/Division Heads Override (15%)
├── District Head Override (15%)
└── Taluka Head DTC Share (30%)
       └── FSE Direct Incentive (allocated from TH share or matching platform bonuses)
```

---

## 📈 2. Commission Percentages by Category

Platform margins and the resulting commission percentages paid to the **Taluka Head DTC** vary by product type:

| Product Category | Avg. Platform Margin | Taluka Head Direct Commission Share |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Software Subscriptions (ERP, LMS)** | 20% to 30% | **8% to 10%** of total contract value |
| **Classroom Hardware (Smart Boards, Projectors)** | 8% to 12% | **3% to 4%** of total order value |
| **Marketplace Goods (Uniforms, Books, Stationers)** | 5% to 10% | **1.5% to 2.5%** of total transacted value |
| **Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)** | 25% to 35% | **10% to 12%** of contract sign-up value |

## 🧮 3. Worked Transaction Examples

### Example A: School Cloud ERP Subscription (Software)
- **School Size**: 600 students.
- **ERP Package Price**: ₹150 per student per year.
- **Total Annual ERP Contract Value**: ₹90,000.
- **Platform Margin**: 30% (₹27,000).
- **Taluka Head Commission (10%)**: **₹9,000** (Paid annually upon subscription renewal).

### Example B: Smart Classroom Upgrade (Hardware Sourcing)
- **Upgrade Package**: 4 Interactive Smart Boards + 4 Projectors.
- **Total Invoice Value**: ₹4,00,000.
- **Platform Margin**: 10% (₹40,000).
- **Taluka Head Commission (4%)**: **₹16,000** (Paid upon installation sign-off).

### Example C: Monthly B2B Marketplace Sourcing (Marketplace Orders)
- **Order Scope**: School Uniform and notebook supply for 1,000 students.
- **Total Marketplace Transaction Value**: ₹8,00,000.
- **Platform Margin**: 6% (₹48,000).
- **Taluka Head Commission (2%)**: **₹16,000** (Paid post-delivery verification).

## 📅 4. Monthly Target & Incentive Plan

Incentives are unlocked when regional DTC performance benchmarks are met:

- **Target Level 1 (Milestone ₹2 Lakh total contract sales in a month)**:
  - Unlocks extra **1% bonus commission** on all hardware transactions closed during that month.
- **Target Level 2 (Onboarding 10 active schools + 5 local vendors in a quarter)**:
  - Unlocks flat **₹15,000 regional growth bonus** to offset local DTC administrative/travel overheads.
