## ⚠️ 3. Risk Assessment & Mitigations

Analyze regional operational hurdles and mitigation strategies:

- **Risk A: Low budget availability in primary schools.**
  - *Mitigation*: Bundle software ERP with free ad-supported modules or focus pitches on smart board packages for secondary classes.
- **Risk B: Poor internet stability in rural villages.**
  - *Mitigation*: Configure offline-first attendance tools and schedule weekly FSE syncs when visiting towns.
- **Risk C: Competitor software pricing matches.**
  - *Mitigation*: Emphasize local physical DTC support and free lab hardware health audits.

## 🤝 4. Mutual Commitments

- **DASP Digital Commits to**:
  - Provision all CRM/ERP credentials within 48 hours of agreement.
  - Dispatch technical engineers for smart board installations within 5 days.
  - Process monthly commission payouts by the 10th of every month.
- **The Taluka Head Commits to**:
  - Maintain a physical DTC office and demo room.
  - Commit at least 25 hours per week to operational duties.
  - Recruit and fund the local FSE field team.
