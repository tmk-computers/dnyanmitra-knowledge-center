## 🏛️ Executive Summary

This pre-signing checklist is used by District Heads to run a verbal confirmation review with the candidate. It ensures that the partner understands the legal boundaries, payment terms, and performance expectations before signing the contract, minimizing future disputes.

---

## ⚖️ Pre-Signing Vetting Checklist

### 1. Responsibilities & Operational Scope
- [ ] **Regional Leadership**: Understands that they manage a local Digital Transformation Centre (DTC) and must recruit their own Field Sales Executives (FSEs).
- [ ] **Active Sourcing**: Agrees to physically audit schools, colleges, and coaching classes using DnyanMitra templates.
- [ ] **Vendor KYC Audits**: Understands that they are responsible for physically inspecting local vendor workshops and retail premises before approving them.

### 2. Revenue Sharing & Payments
- [ ] **Commission Metrics**: Confirms they understand that payouts depend on product types (e.g. 10% on software subscriptions, 4% on hardware setups, 2% on marketplace sales).
- [ ] **Settlement Cycle**: Agrees to the monthly settlement cycle (commission statements generated by the 5th, payouts settled by the 10th).
- [ ] **Tax & Deductions**: Understands that all payouts are subject to government TDS guidelines.

### 3. Exclusivity & Territory Boundaries
- [ ] **Territory Limits**: Understands that they hold exclusive rights for school deals within their allocated taluka only.
- [ ] **Non-Compete**: Agrees not to promote or represent competing EdTech, ERP, or school procurement marketplace platforms during the contract term.

### 4. Code of Conduct & Safety
- [ ] **Zero-Kickback Policy**: Agrees never to demand or accept commissions, gifts, or incentives from local vendors or school administrators.
- [ ] **Data Security**: Understands that student directories, fee collection logs, and school databases are confidential.

### 5. Termination & Handover Policy
- [ ] **Notice Period**: Understands that either party can terminate the agreement by providing 30 days written notice.
- [ ] **Client Handover**: Agrees that upon termination, all active client school logs and vendor files must be handed over to the District Head.
