## 🔎 Vendor Physical Audit Checklist

To prevent fraudulent listings or shell retail setups, the partner must physically inspect all vendor premises:

- [ ] **Address Check**: Verify physical address of vendor's workshop or warehouse.
- [ ] **Stock Vetting**: Confirm vendor has stock on hand (stationery, desks, uniforms).
- [ ] **PAN & GSTIN Check**: Match details with business registration sheets.
- [ ] **Photo Log**: Take front storefront and stock area photo with CRM camera.

---

## 🏁 Review Checklist
- [ ] Reject vendors lacking storefront signage or operating from residential units.
