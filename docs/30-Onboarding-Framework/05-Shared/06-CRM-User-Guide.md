## 📱 CRM Navigation Steps

### 1. Adding a School Lead
- Click **[Add Lead]** button on home screen.
- Confirm GPS location is active (coordinates auto-populate).
- Input school name, principal phone, and target student count.

### 2. Submitting Audit Survey
- Navigate to **[Audit Survey]** tab in school profile.
- Select checkboxes for smart boards, computer counts, and CCTV.
- Click **[Submit Audit]** to trigger automated tech report.
