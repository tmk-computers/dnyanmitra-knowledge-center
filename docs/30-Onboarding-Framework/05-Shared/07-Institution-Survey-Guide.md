## 🔍 On-Site Audit Methodology

### 1. Infrastructure Checks
- Count existing smart boards and functional PCs.
- Test school internet bandwidth speed via CRM utility tool.

### 2. Safety Compliance
- Check CCTV placement (log blind spots).
- Confirm presence of school bus GPS trackers.

### 3. Reporting
- Complete all fields in CRM before leaving campus.
