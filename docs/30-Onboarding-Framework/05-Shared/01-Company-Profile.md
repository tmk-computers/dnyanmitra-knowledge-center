## 🏛️ Executive Summary
DASP Digital is a Pune-based EdTech and SportsTech company specializing in localized B2B procurement networks and institutional cloud software ERP systems.

## 📈 Five-Year Expansion Plan
- **Phase 1 (Year 1)**: Deploy DTC model in 40 districts of Maharashtra.
- **Phase 2 (Year 2)**: Launch KrushiMitra agricultural marketplace.
- **Phase 3 (Year 3)**: Establish AI grading engines across all partner schools.
- **Phase 4 (Year 4)**: Expand municipal procurement (ZilaValley).
