## 📂 FAQs Section
- **Q**: What is the commission payout date?  
  **A**: Reconciled by the 5th and settled by the 10th of every month.
- **Q**: Who provides L1 technical support?  
  **A**: The local Taluka DTC coordinates L1 support; escalations are logged in the CRM helpdesk.
