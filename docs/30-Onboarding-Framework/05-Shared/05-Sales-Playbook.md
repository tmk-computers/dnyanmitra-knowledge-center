## 🔄 Sales Progression Stages

1. **Mapping**: Identify schools and principal names. Log details in CRM.
2. **Demo Booking**: Call principal to schedule a 20-minute product demonstration.
3. **IX Tech Audit**: Visit campus, check computers, security, and present audit findings.
4. **Negotiation**: Draft customized pricing proposal for board approval.
5. **Closing**: HQ generates contract and billing receipt post-agreement.
