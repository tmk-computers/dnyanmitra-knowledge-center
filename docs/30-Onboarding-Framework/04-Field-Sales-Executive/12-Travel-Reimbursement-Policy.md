## 🚗 Expense Reimbursement Rules

- **Approved Mode**: Travel is approved for two-wheelers only.
- **Rate**: Reimbursement is calculated at a rate of ₹3.50 per kilometer.
- **Monthly Cap**: Capped at a maximum of ₹3,000 per month.
- **Claim Process**: FSE must log start and end odometer readings alongside the school name in the CRM daily. 
- **Submit Date**: Submit travel logs on the 1st of every month for reimbursement.
