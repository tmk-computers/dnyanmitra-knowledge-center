## 🏛️ Executive Summary
The FSE is the primary field agent of DnyanMitra, responsible for identifying prospects and mapping local educational databases.

## 📋 Core Job Responsibilities

- **Daily School Visits**: Complete 3 to 4 physical visits daily.
- **CRM Logs**: Log school GPS location, contact name, and phone numbers in the CRM.
- **Institutional Surveys**: Conduct on-site audits of school computers, smart boards, and CCTV.
- **Vendor Audits**: Verify local stationery/IT vendor locations.
- **Support Coordination**: Follow up on customer support tickets in the taluka.
