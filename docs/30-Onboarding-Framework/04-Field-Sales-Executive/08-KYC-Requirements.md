## 🔎 KYC Verification Steps

- **Aadhaar Verification**: Confirm identity matches the applicant's PAN card.
- **Address Verification**: Review rent agreement or utility bill copy.
- **Reference Checks**: Contact at least one previous manager to confirm job performance and conduct.
- **Driving Licence Check**: Verify status of active licence.
