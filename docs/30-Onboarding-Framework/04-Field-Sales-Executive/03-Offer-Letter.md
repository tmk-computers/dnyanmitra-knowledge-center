## 📄 Offer Letter Layout

Date: YYYY-MM-DD  
To,  
**Mr./Ms. [Candidate Name]**,  
**[Candidate Address]**

Subject: Offer of Employment as Field Sales Executive.

Dear [Candidate Name],

Following your interview with the **[Taluka Name]** Digital Transformation Centre (DTC), we are pleased to offer you the position of **Field Sales Executive (FSE)**.

### Compensation & Conditions:
- **Base Salary**: ₹15,000 per month (fixed).
- **Travel Allowance**: ₹3,000 per month (based on CRM log logs).
- **Incentives**: Eligible for performance incentives based on sales targets.
- **Probation Period**: 3 months.

Please sign the duplicate copy of this letter within 3 business days to confirm your acceptance.

Sincerely,

**Taluka Head Coordinator**  
[Taluka Name] DTC
