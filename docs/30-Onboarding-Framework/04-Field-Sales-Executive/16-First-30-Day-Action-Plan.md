## 📅 Weekly Field Action Plan

- Introduce DnyanMitra and log school principal contact info.

### Week 3: Vendor Vetting Drive
- Visit 10 local B2B vendors (IT shops, stationers).
- Conduct physical KYC and take photo logs of premises.

### Week 4: Month 1 Progress Review
- Review CRM logs with Taluka Head.
- Map target routes for Month 2.
