## 📱 Mobile Device Guidelines

- **Standard Device**: FSEs must use their personal Android smartphone (OS 10 or higher).
- **Mobile Allowance**: A monthly telecom package allowance of ₹300 is provided.
- **CRM App Security**: FSE must keep the CRM app updated and use secure PIN/fingerprint locks.
- **Data Protection**: Do not share screen caps of CRM lead charts on personal messaging channels.
