## 🏁 Launch checklist

- [ ] **Employment Agreement**: Signed and uploaded to CRM.
- [ ] **KYC Verification**: Aadhaar and PAN checks completed.
- [ ] **Bank Details**: Pennydrop test completed.
- [ ] **CRM App Setup**: Verified active login on employee's mobile.
- [ ] **Asset Issue**: ID Card and branded apparel issued.
- [ ] **Initial Training**: Completed modules TR-FSE-01 through 04.

---

## 🏁 Review Checklist
- [ ] Obtain Taluka Head coordinator sign-off before scheduling the first school route.
