## ⚖️ Employment Contract Clauses

### 1. Scope of Work
- Execute daily school visits, log GPS coordinates, conduct digital audits, and update CRM dashboards.

### 2. Salary & Payout
- Fixed Monthly Salary: ₹15,000. Travel Allowance: ₹3,000. Payout by 10th of every month.

### 3. Working Hours & Leave
- Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Eligible for 1 casual leave per month with prior approval.

### 4. Notice Period & Probation
- Notice period: 15 days written notice by either party. Probation period: 90 days.
