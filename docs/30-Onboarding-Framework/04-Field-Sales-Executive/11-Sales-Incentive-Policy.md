## 💰 Performance Incentive Tiers

Incentives are paid monthly on closed institutional sales:

- **Tier 1 (School ERP)**: ₹1,500 per school deployment closed.
- **Tier 2 (Smart Boards)**: ₹1,000 per smart classroom installation package.
- **Tier 3 (B2B Vendor)**: ₹500 per verified vendor onboarded to the B2B marketplace.
- **Monthly Bonus**: Close 5 school ERP deals in a month to unlock a flat ₹5,000 performance bonus.
