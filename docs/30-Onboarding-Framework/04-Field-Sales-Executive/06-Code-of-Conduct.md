## 🚫 Field Conduct Standards

- **Zero Tolerance on Kickbacks**: Requesting money, commissions, or gifts from local suppliers is strictly prohibited.
- **Client Respect**: Professional and polite interaction with school principals and staff.
- **Data Protection**: Do not download, copy, or share school database logs.
- **Dress Code**: Formal trousers, collared shirt, and clean shoes. Official DnyanMitra ID card must be visible.
