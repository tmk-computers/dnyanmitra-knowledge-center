## 🔒 Confidentiality Guidelines

### 1. Scope of Confidential Information
- Includes student registries, fee collection records, parent contact sheets, vendor pricing contracts, and CRM source lists.

### 2. Restrictive Covenants
- The partner agrees not to download bulk lists or share access credentials with external IT agencies.

### 3. Breach Consequences
- Breach of confidentiality results in immediate contract termination, delisting, and legal recovery actions under Pune jurisdiction.
