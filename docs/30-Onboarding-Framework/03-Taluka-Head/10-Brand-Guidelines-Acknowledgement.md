## 🎨 Branding Quality Standards

- **Color Palettes**: Primary logo uses deep blue (HEX #143C78) and orange (HEX #F58220).
- **Logo Modifiers**: No stretching, color shifts, or addition of background graphics.
- **Office Signage**: DTC office must display the standardized DnyanMitra board size (6x3 feet).
- **Collateral Use**: All printed flyers or local ads must use templates provided in the CRM library.
