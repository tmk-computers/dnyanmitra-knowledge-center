## 🏛️ Executive Summary
To prevent fraud and maintain institutional compliance, all files below must be submitted and validated.

## 🗂️ Required Documents Tracker

- [ ] **Aadhaar Card**: Checked for correct name mapping matching bank logs.
- [ ] **PAN Card**: Required for TDS compliance processing.
- [ ] **Passport Photo**: Clean front face capture for CRM identity profile.
- [ ] **Cancelled Cheque**: Checked for active IFS code alignment.
- [ ] **Office Rent/Ownership Proof**: Copy of registry index or active rent agreement.
- [ ] **Highest Education Degree Certificate**: Copy of diploma or degree sheet.
- [ ] **GSTIN Certificate** (Optional): Required if applicant bills through a firm.

## 🏁 Review Checklist
- [ ] Ensure photocopies are clean and legible before scan uploading.
- [ ] Check names across all documents match exactly.
