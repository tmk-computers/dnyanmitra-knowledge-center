## 🏛️ Executive Summary
All DnyanMitra representatives must maintain the highest integrity levels, protecting user privacy and avoiding commercial conflicts of interest.

## 🚫 Core Policy Standards

- **Zero-Kickback Rule**: Demanding commissions or gifts from B2B marketplace vendors is strictly prohibited.
- **Ethical Sales**: No false revenue or product performance promises to school administrators.
- **Conflict of Interest**: Partners cannot run or represent competing EdTech or B2B platforms.
- **Dress Code**: Professional smart casual wear with official DASP identity cards when visiting schools.
