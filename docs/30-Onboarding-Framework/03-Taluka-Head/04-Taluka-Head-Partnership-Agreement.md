## ⚖️ Core Contract Clauses

### 1. Appointment & Territory
- DASP Digital appoints the partner as the exclusive Digital Transformation Centre (DTC) operator for the specified taluka territory.

### 2. Operational Obligations
- The partner must maintain a physical office, recruit field executives, audit schools, and verify B2B vendor locations.

### 3. Commissions & Revenue Splits
- Commissions are paid on software ERP (10%), hardware setups (4%), and marketplace transactions (2%), subject to standard tax withholding.

### 4. Notice Period & Termination
- Either party can terminate this agreement by providing a 30-day written notice. Immediate termination applies for code breaches.
