## 🏛️ Executive Summary
The onboarding process bridges candidate selection and active operations, ensuring all hires and partners are fully aligned with the B2B marketplace model.

## 🎯 Role Definitions & Goals

### 1. Taluka Head (TH)
- **Role Type**: Franchise Partner / Entrepreneur.
- **Objective**: Establish and lead the regional Digital Transformation Centre (DTC).
- **Core Focus**: Institution and vendor relations, FSE team guidance.

### 2. Field Sales Executive (FSE)
- **Role Type**: Salaried employee hired by the Taluka Head.
- **Objective**: Conduct daily on-site school audits and database mapping.
- **Core Focus**: CRM log entry, lead routing, and local customer support.
