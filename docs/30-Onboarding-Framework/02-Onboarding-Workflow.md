## 🏛️ Executive Summary
This document charts out the progression roadmap from first candidate identification to 90-day review.

## 🔄 Onboarding Lifecycle Flowchart

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[1. Candidate ID] --> B[2. Initial Outreach Call]
    B --> C[3. 1st Demo Meeting]
    C --> D[4. 2nd Business Workshop]
    D --> E[5. Due Diligence KYC Check]
    E --> F[6. 3rd Onboarding Meeting]
    F --> G[7. Agreement Executed]
    G --> H[8. Docs Verification]
    H --> I[9. Bank Pennydrop Check]
    I --> J[10. Training Program]
    J --> K[11. Knowledge Assessment]
    K --> L[12. CRM Dashboard Provision]
    L --> M[13. Coordinator Email Setup]
    M --> N[14. Welcome Kit Sent]
    N --> O[15. DTC Active Launch]
    O --> P[16. 30/90 Day Review PIP]
```
